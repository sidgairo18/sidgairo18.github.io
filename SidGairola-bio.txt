Siddhartha Gairola is a Researcher at Adobe Inc. in the Media and Data Science Research Group. Previously he was a Research Assistant at the Center for Visual Information Technology, IIIT-Hyderabad. He has completed his Master's and B.Tech with Honours in Computer Science from IIIT-Hyderabad and was advised by Prof. P.J. Narayanan. His research interests include Computer Vision, Image Processing, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
Siddhartha also works as an open source software developer and has participated in Google Summer of Code as a developer for 2 years running 2017 and 2018 for the organization Scilab.
